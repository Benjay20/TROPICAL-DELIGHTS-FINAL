Background History

Our business started with the help of my siblings, who generously supported us until we saved enough to start on our own. We began selling fruits and vegetables from a small cart, and eventually, we were able to rent a proper stall to showcase our goods. Over time, we hired our nieces and nephews to help us run the business. This venture has been a blessing, especially when it came to supporting the education of my children. Thanks to this business, we've managed to overcome many challenges and provide for our family.

Our Fruits

  • Apple

    Crisp and juicy, apples are a popular snack, rich in fiber and vitamin C, available in various colors and flavors.

  • Banana

    Soft and sweet, bananas are a convenient source of energy, high in potassium, and great for smoothies or as a quick snack.

  • Lanzones

    Lanzones are tropical fruits with a sweet, tangy flavor and a translucent flesh, often enjoyed fresh or in desserts.

  • Papaya

    Sweet and aromatic, papayas are packed with vitamins A and C, and their soft, orange flesh is perfect for salads and smoothies.

  • Pear

    Pears are sweet and juicy, with a smooth texture, available in several varieties, making them a delightful addition to salads and desserts.

  • Pineapple

    Tropical and tangy, pineapples are juicy fruits rich in vitamin C, often used in drinks, desserts, and savory dishes..

  • Watermelon

    Refreshing and hydrating, watermelons are large, sweet fruits filled with water, perfect for summer picnics and fruit salads.
